# CirrusLabs - ICP Framework

**Offer:** Talent As A Service (TaaS) - Staffing for Data, AI, Cloud, and Software Development roles
**Regions:** US (primary) and LATAM (secondary)
**Sales Models:** Individual placements or Pod selling (team delivery)

---

## Company Profile (Where We Find Opportunities)

### Target Size Categories

#### 1. Startups (Highest Intent)
- **Funding stage:** Series A and above ($20M+ raised)
- **Why:** Funded startups are hiring, have approved budgets, fast decision-making
- **Typical headcount:** 20-500 employees
- **Hiring volume:** Often 3-8 engineering roles at once
- **Decision speed:** Fast (1-2 weeks)

#### 2. SMBs (High Intent)
- **Employee range:** 50-1,000 employees
- **Revenue stage:** Typically $5M-$100M ARR
- **Why:** Growth stage, need experienced engineers quickly
- **Hiring volume:** Usually 1-3 roles per quarter
- **Decision speed:** Medium (2-4 weeks)

#### 3. Enterprises (Lower Intent, Higher Deal Size)
- **Employee range:** 1,000+ employees
- **Why:** Scaling teams, budget approved, volume orders possible
- **Typical need:** Platform teams, infrastructure, AI initiatives
- **Hiring volume:** Can be 5-15 roles for pod selling
- **Decision speed:** Slow (4-8 weeks)

### Geographic Scope
- **Primary:** United States (all regions, emphasis on tech hubs)
- **Secondary:** LATAM
  - Brazil (most engineers available)
  - Mexico (growth stage startups)
  - Colombia (emerging market)

> **Note:** Geography scope defined here is the system default. Individual vertical playbooks (`context/verticals/{slug}/icp.md`) override this for their specific market. The staffing vertical targets US + Singapore + UK; the system default (Americas) applies when no vertical is set.

## Company ICP Scoring Rubric

### Scoring Criteria (Points Add Up)

| Criteria | Points | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| **Active hiring signal** | +100 | Job posting for Data/AI/Cloud/SW found in last 7 days |
| Company size in range | +50 | Startups: Series A+, SMBs: 50-1000, Enterprises: 1000+ |
| Growth trajectory** | +30 | 10%+ YoY growth in headcount |
| Sufficient funding** | +20 | Startups: Series A+; SMBs/Enterprise: revenue-backed |
| Tech-forward** | +20 | Uses modern stack (Cloud, AI/ML platforms) |
| Geographic match | +15 | US (highest), LATAM (secondary) |

**Minimum Score to Target: 170 points**

### Scoring Explanation
- **100 points = Active hiring signal** - This alone puts you above 170
- **50 points = Right size company** - They can afford us
- **20 points each = Growth/tech/funding** - Supports hiring intent

**Example:**
- Startup found hiring Data Engineers (100) + Series A funded (20) + In US (15) = **135 points** (below threshold)
- SMB with 200 employees (50) + Hiring Data Engineer (100) + 15% growth (30) + Uses AWS/Databricks (20) = **200 points** ✅ TARGET

## Company Disqualifiers (Automatic Filter-Out)

❌ **Hard stops:**
- NOT actively hiring for Data/AI/Cloud/Software Dev roles
- Fewer than 50 employees (too small, no budget)
- Series Seed funding only (not approved budgets yet)
- Outside US/LATAM
- No identifiable decision-maker
- Recruiting agency or staffing firm (they're competitors)

❌ **Soft stops** (reconsider based on context):
- Very early stage (<$5M funding, pre-Series A) - might not have budget
- Over 5000 employees (very slow decision cycles)
- Non-tech companies (different buyer psychology)

## Buyer Profile (Who Makes the Decision)

### ICP Titles (In Preference Order)

#### 1. CTO (Best)
- **Authority:** Full hiring control + budget ownership
- **Motivation:** Building the technical foundation
- **Response rate:** ~50% (highest)
- **Decision speed:** Fast (1-2 weeks)
- **Example pain:** "We need 2 more senior engineers but can't find them"
- **Pod selling fit:** Perfect (understands team dynamics)

#### 2. VP of Engineering / VP Engineering
- **Authority:** Own the entire engineering org
- **Motivation:** Deliver on product roadmap, staffing capacity
- **Response rate:** ~35%
- **Decision speed:** Medium (2-3 weeks)
- **Pod selling fit:** Good (think in terms of squads)

#### 3. Director of Engineering
- **Authority:** Hiring authority for their team
- **Motivation:** Ship features, manage their team's growth
- **Response rate:** ~25%
- **Decision speed:** Medium (2-4 weeks)
- **Pod selling fit:** Good (know exact role needs)

#### 4. Director of Data / Data Platform
- **Authority:** Can hire for data roles specifically
- **Motivation:** Build data infrastructure, enable analytics
- **Response rate:** ~30% (for data-specific roles)
- **Decision speed:** Medium
- **Pod selling fit:** Excellent (data projects are teams)

#### 5. CIO (Enterprise-specific)
- **Authority:** Infrastructure + modernization budgets
- **Motivation:** Digital transformation, modernize stack
- **Response rate:** ~20% (very busy)
- **Decision speed:** Slow (4-8 weeks)
- **Pod selling fit:** Excellent (cloud migration projects)

#### 6. Founder / Co-founder (Startups)
- **Authority:** Full decision-making power
- **Motivation:** Move fast, get best talent
- **Response rate:** ~10-15% (very busy, get lots of outreach)
- **Decision speed:** Fast when they reply (48 hours)
- **Pod selling fit:** Perfect (think bigger picture)
- **Warning:** Don't cold email founders - too much noise. Use other titles first.

### Why These Titles?
✅ Have P&L authority or direct hiring control
✅ Understand engineering needs (not HR gatekeepers)
✅ Can approve budgets without layers of approval
✅ Feel the pain of unfilled roles directly

## Vertical Sub-ICPs (Different Angles for Different Buys)

### Data Modernization Vertical
- **Company signal:** Using legacy databases (Oracle, SQL Server on-prem)
- **Buyer signal:** "Evaluating cloud migration" or "Building data platform"
- **Pod size:** Usually 2-4 Data Engineers
- **Sales angle:** "We place teams that modernize data infrastructure"

### AI/ML Vertical
- **Company signal:** Hiring for "ML Engineer", "ML Operations", "MLOps"
- **Buyer signal:** "Building AI capabilities" or "Scaling ML team"
- **Pod size:** Usually 2-3 ML Engineers + 1 ML Ops
- **Sales angle:** "We build out ML teams for companies like you"

### Cloud / Infrastructure Vertical
- **Company signal:** "Hiring Cloud Architect", "Platform Engineer"
- **Buyer signal:** "Cloud migration" or "Building platform team"
- **Pod size:** 2-4 Cloud Engineers
- **Sales angle:** "We staff cloud infrastructure projects"

### General Software Development
- **Company signal:** "Hiring Software Engineer", "Senior Backend Engineer"
- **Buyer signal:** "Scaling engineering" or "Product roadmap needs"
- **Pod size:** 1-8 engineers depending on company
- **Sales angle:** "Flexible staffing for product teams"

---

## Key Principles

1. **Hiring signal is the best predictor** - If someone is actively posting jobs, they need people NOW
2. **Size matters** - Too small (no budget), too large (slow decisions)
3. **Buyer title matters more than company stage** - A CTO at a 30-person startup can say yes faster than an HR person at a 5000-person company
4. **Pod selling requires minimum 2-3 roles** - We identify this in the hiring signals and adjust pitch angle
5. **Geographic focus simplifies** - US first, LATAM second
