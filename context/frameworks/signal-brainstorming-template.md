## Signal Hypothesis

> Complete this sentence: "Companies that are [SIGNAL] probably need [WHAT WE OFFER] because [REASON]."

**Example:**
> Companies that are *actively hiring Data Engineers for 30+ days* probably need *our pre-vetted talent pipeline* because *they're struggling to find qualified candidates fast enough*.

**Your hypothesis:**
> Companies that are _____________ probably need _____________ because _____________.

---

## Signal Validation Checklist

Answer these before building the campaign:

- [ ] **Observable?** Can you detect this signal via Apollo, LinkedIn, or news?
- [ ] **Timely?** Is the signal fresh (< 30 days)? Older signals = colder leads
- [ ] **Predictive?** Does this signal predict budget and intent?
- [ ] **Scalable?** Are there 100+ companies with this signal?
- [ ] **Targetable?** Can you reach the decision-maker at these companies?

## Messaging Framework Selection

Pick the framework that fits your signal:

| Framework | Best For | Example Opening |
|-----------|----------|----------------|
| Pain → Agitate → Solve | Clear, acute pain | "Hiring a Data Engineer is taking 3-4 months..." |
| Before → After → Bridge | Transformation story | "Before CirrusLabs, companies spent 90 days..." |
| Curiosity | When pain is not obvious | "How are companies scaling their data teams 3x faster?" |
| Case Study | When you have proof | "We placed 5 engineers at [Company] in 3 weeks..." |
| Problem → Solution | Simple, direct | "Your team is blocked. Here's how we unblock it." |
