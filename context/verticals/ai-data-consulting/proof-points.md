# AI & Data Consulting — Proof Points

> Credible evidence patterns for AI/data consulting engagements. Use bracketed placeholders for client-specific claims; use capability statements as-is.

---

## Capability Statements (Reusable As-Is)

### AI/ML Strategy & Roadmapping
- Cross-functional AI readiness assessments covering data infrastructure, talent, and governance
- Technology-agnostic ML strategy — we recommend the right stack, not the one we resell
- Executive-ready AI roadmaps with phased investment milestones and success metrics
- Hands-on proof-of-concept development to de-risk AI investments before scaling

### Data Platform Modernization
- End-to-end migration from legacy data warehouses to modern lakehouse architectures
- Real-time streaming pipeline design and implementation (Kafka, Spark, Flink)
- Data mesh and domain-oriented data ownership implementation
- Cost optimization for cloud data platforms — typical savings of 30-50% on compute spend

### Analytics & BI Modernization
- Self-service analytics platform design that reduces analyst dependency by 60%+
- Semantic layer implementation for consistent metrics across business units
- Migration from legacy BI tools to modern stack (dbt + cloud warehouse + modern visualization)
- Data quality frameworks with automated monitoring, lineage tracking, and SLA alerting

### GenAI & LLM Applications
- RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) architecture design and implementation
- LLM evaluation frameworks for accuracy, cost, and latency optimization
- Responsible AI guardrails — bias detection, output filtering, compliance controls
- Custom fine-tuning pipelines for domain-specific language models

- "Our team includes former [notable company type] data/ML engineers"

- "[N] companies in [industry] have modernized their data stack with our guidance"
- "Referred by [role] at [peer company type] after their successful engagement"
- "Our NPS among data leaders is [score] — measured post-engagement"

- "We start with a 2-week diagnostic — you see exactly what we would build before committing"
- "Fixed-scope proof of concept with clear success criteria before scaling"
- "Knowledge transfer is built into every engagement — we leave your team stronger"
- "All IP and code produced during the engagement belongs to you from day one"

## Objection-Defusing Proof Patterns

| Objection | Proof Response Pattern |
|-----------|----------------------|
| "We can hire data scientists directly" | "Our last [N] clients tried that first — average time to first production model was [X] months internal vs [Y] weeks with our team. We also transfer knowledge so your hires ramp faster." |
| "We already have a data team" | "We augment, not replace. In [recent engagement], we worked alongside [N] internal engineers and left them owning the entire platform independently." |
| "AI projects have high failure rates" | "Industry average is 87% failure. Our structured approach — diagnostic first, then POC with clear success criteria — delivers production models in [timeframe]. We do not start what we cannot finish." |
| "Consulting is too expensive" | "Our diagnostic engagement is [price range]. Compare that to [months] of internal iteration. One client spent [X] months internally before engaging us — we shipped in [Y] weeks." |
| "We need someone who knows our industry" | "Data infrastructure patterns are 80% universal. We have built platforms for [industry types]. The 20% that is domain-specific, we learn in the first 2 weeks of diagnostic." |
