## Capability Statements (Reusable As-Is)

### Speed to Fill
- Pre-vetted bench of contract-ready engineers reduces typical sourcing timeline from weeks to days
- Parallel candidate sourcing: multiple qualified profiles presented simultaneously, not sequentially
- Same-week shortlist delivery for standard engineering roles (backend, frontend, data, DevOps)

### Quality & Retention
- Multi-stage technical vetting: resume screen → coding assessment → system design → culture fit interview
- Contractor retention rates consistently above industry average due to matching methodology
- Replacement guarantee on all placements — if a contractor doesn't work out within the first 30 days, we re-source at no additional cost

### Flexibility & Scale
- Contract, contract-to-hire, and direct placement models — choose what fits your budget cycle
- Scale teams up or down without long-term headcount commitments
- Cross-timezone coverage: engineers available in US, APAC, and EMEA time zones

### Domain Depth
- Specialization in high-demand engineering disciplines: data engineering, ML/AI, cloud infrastructure, full-stack development
- Recruiters who understand the technical stack — can speak to candidates about frameworks, not just keywords
- Hiring manager satisfaction: we source for technical fit, not just keyword match

## Social Proof Patterns

### Quantitative (Use When You Have Real Numbers)
- "We've placed [N]+ engineers across [M] companies in [industry/region] over the past [timeframe]"
- "[N]% of our placements pass the 90-day mark — well above the [X]% industry average"
- "Average time from kickoff to accepted offer: [X] business days"

### Qualitative (Reusable Without Modification)
- "Our recruiters have an engineering background — they can evaluate candidates on technical depth, not just resume keywords"
- "We maintain a pre-vetted bench of contract-ready engineers so you're not starting from zero"
- "Every placement comes with a replacement guarantee — we stand behind our matches"

### Authority Signals (Reusable Without Modification)
- Deep specialization in technical hiring — not a generalist staffing agency
- Track record with both high-growth startups and enterprise engineering teams
- Active in US and APAC markets with local sourcing presence

## Metrics Reference Table

| Metric | What It Measures | Good Benchmark | How to Cite |
|--------|-----------------|----------------|-------------|
| Time to shortlist | Days from kickoff to first qualified candidates | < 5 business days | "We typically present shortlisted candidates within one business week" |
| Interview-to-offer ratio | % of interviews that result in offers | > 40% | "Our pre-vetting means fewer wasted interviews" |
| 90-day retention | % of placements still active at 90 days | > 90% | "Over 9 in 10 of our placements pass the 90-day mark" |
| Contract-to-hire conversion | % of contractors converted to FTE | > 50% | "More than half of our contractors get converted — a sign of match quality" |
| Replacement rate | % of placements requiring replacement | < 10% | "We rarely need to activate our replacement guarantee" |
| Client NPS | Net Promoter Score from hiring managers | > 50 | "Our hiring manager satisfaction scores consistently exceed industry benchmarks" |
