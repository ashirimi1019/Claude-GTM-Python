# Outreach Instructions for Instantly

## Setup

1. Go to [Instantly.ai](https://instantly.ai)
2. Create a new campaign
3. Upload this CSV: `messages.csv`

## CSV Columns

- **first_name** - Recipient first name
- **last_name** - Recipient last name
- **email** - Email to send to
- **company** - Company name (for tracking)
- **subject** - Email subject line
- **body** - Email body
- **variant** - Which email variant (for A/B testing analysis)

## Campaign Settings (Recommended)

- **Warm-up:** Enable (builds sender reputation)
- **Rate limit:** 5-10 emails per hour (safe limit)
- **Business hours:** 9am-5pm recipient timezone
- **Reply handling:** Auto-pause on reply
- **Follow-ups:** Set to 3-5 day intervals
