# Campaign Strategy — Hiring Engineers ATL SMB Q1 2026

## Campaign Name
Hiring Engineers - Atlanta SMB Q1 2026

## Signal Hypothesis
SMB companies (50-500 employees) in the Atlanta metro area actively hiring for Software Engineers, Data Engineers, DevOps Engineers, and related technical roles are likely experiencing talent acquisition challenges and would benefit from CirrusLabs's staffing services.

## Target Geography
Georgia, United States

## Target Company Profile
- **Size:** 50-500 employees (SMB)
- **Location:** Atlanta, GA metro area
- **Industry:** Technology, SaaS, FinTech, HealthTech, Logistics, E-Commerce
- **Funding:** Series A through Series C, or bootstrapped with revenue
- **Signal:** Actively hiring 2+ engineering roles

## Buyer Personas
1. **VP of Engineering** — Feels the pain of unfilled roles directly; owns headcount budget
2. **CTO** — Strategic hire decisions; cares about speed-to-fill and talent quality
3. **Director of Engineering** — Day-to-day hiring manager; needs immediate relief
4. **Head of Talent / HR** — Owns recruiting process; evaluates staffing partners

## Key Messages
1. Atlanta SMBs lose top engineers to FAANG remote offers — CirrusLabs gives you access to pre-vetted talent that wants to work in Atlanta
2. Reduce time-to-fill from 3-6 months to 3-4 weeks
3. No upfront cost — pay only when you hire

## Sequence Strategy
- 3-step email sequence over 10 days
- Personalized first touch referencing specific open roles
- Follow-up with Atlanta tech ecosystem proof points
- LinkedIn connection request as parallel channel

## Success Metrics
- Open rate > 40%
- Reply rate > 8%
- Meeting booked rate > 3%
