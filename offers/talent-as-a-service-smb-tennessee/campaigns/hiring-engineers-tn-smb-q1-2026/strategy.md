# Campaign Strategy — Hiring Engineers TN SMB Q1 2026

## Campaign Name
Hiring Engineers - Tennessee SMB Q1 2026

## Signal Hypothesis
SMB companies (50-500 employees) in Tennessee actively hiring for Software Engineers, Data Engineers, DevOps Engineers, and related technical roles are likely experiencing talent acquisition challenges and would benefit from CirrusLabs's staffing services.

## Target Geography
Tennessee, United States

## Target Company Profile
- **Size:** 50-500 employees (SMB)
- **Location:** Tennessee (Nashville, Memphis, Knoxville, Chattanooga)
- **Industry:** Technology, SaaS, HealthTech, FinTech, Logistics, E-Commerce
- **Funding:** Series A through Series C, or bootstrapped with revenue
- **Signal:** Actively hiring 2+ engineering roles

## Buyer Personas
1. **VP of Engineering** — Feels the pain of unfilled roles directly; owns headcount budget
2. **CTO** — Strategic hire decisions; cares about speed-to-fill and talent quality
3. **Director of Engineering** — Day-to-day hiring manager; needs immediate relief
4. **Head of Talent / HR** — Owns recruiting process; evaluates staffing partners

## Key Messages
1. Tennessee's tech boom means more competition for engineers — CirrusLabs gives you access to pre-vetted talent that wants to work in TN
2. Reduce time-to-fill from 3-6 months to 3-4 weeks
3. No upfront cost — pay only when you hire

## Sequence Strategy
- 3-step email sequence over 10 days
- Personalized first touch referencing specific open roles
- Follow-up with Tennessee tech ecosystem proof points
- LinkedIn connection request as parallel channel

## Success Metrics
- Open rate > 40%
- Reply rate > 8%
- Meeting booked rate > 3%
