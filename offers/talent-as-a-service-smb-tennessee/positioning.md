# Positioning Canvas — Talent as a Service - SMB Tennessee

## 1. Category
Staffing / Talent as a Service

## 2. Target Customer
SMB companies (50-500 employees) in Tennessee (Nashville, Memphis, Knoxville, Chattanooga) that are actively hiring software engineers, data engineers, or DevOps roles

## 3. Customer Problem
Tennessee's tech scene is growing fast but SMBs struggle to compete with remote FAANG offers and Nashville's booming healthcare tech sector for engineering talent. Open roles stay unfilled for 3-6 months.

## 4. Why Now
Nashville is one of the fastest-growing tech hubs in the Southeast. Companies are scaling engineering teams but local talent supply hasn't caught up with demand.

## 5. Customer Alternative
In-house recruiting, LinkedIn job posts, or traditional staffing agencies with no tech specialization

## 6. Observable Success
Hire vetted engineers in 3-4 weeks instead of months

## 7. Unique Capabilities
CirrusLabs provides pre-vetted, immediately deployable engineering talent with domain expertise in cloud, data, and AI

## 8. Offer Description
We place full-time and contract engineers with deep expertise in AI, cloud, and data engineering — focused on Tennessee's growing tech market

## 9. Key Differentiators
- Pre-vetted talent pool with domain-specific expertise
- Signal-driven approach identifies companies before they post jobs publicly
- Southeast-focused network of engineers who want to work in Tennessee
- Deep understanding of Nashville/Memphis tech ecosystems
